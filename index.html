In the Grim Darkness of the Far Future...

Warhammer 40,000 is a dystopian science-fantasy tabletop miniature wargame produced by Games Workshop. Set in the 41st millennium, it features massive battles between various factions such as the Imperium of Man, Chaos, Orks, Eldar, and more.

Featured Faction: Space Marines

Genetically modified superhuman soldiers serving the Emperor of Mankind, Space Marines are one of the most iconic factions in the 40k universe.
